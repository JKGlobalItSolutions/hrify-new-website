Employee

Dashboard

The Pilamar MD Dashboard acts as a centralized command center for HR teams. It offers an intuitive calendar view to track employee attendance, showing check-ins, check-outs, and total working hours in real time.

Color-coded indicators make it easy to identify present, absent, or on-leave employees. Managers receive instant alerts for issues like location permission errors, ensuring smooth and accurate data tracking.

Quick action buttons like "Punch Out" and "Take Break" simplify everyday workflows for employees, while quick links to Attendance, Leave, and Payroll modules allow HR teams to navigate efficiently.
